Experiences in Italy...

1. Visit the Colosseum and explore the amphitheater’s first and second levels while learning about gruesome gladiator battles. Then visit the Roman Forum to see its ancient ruins like the Temple of Julius Caesar.

2. Visit Torcello, Murano and Burano — three islands are all in Venice. Here you can watch how glass is made at a Murano glass factory, see historical cathedrals on Torcello and admire exquisite handmade lace on Burano.

3. Visit Lake Como to see its\ dramatic scenery, set against the foothills of the Alps.
